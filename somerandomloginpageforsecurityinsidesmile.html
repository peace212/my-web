You Are special

And I will bring you back from wherever you go

Yes you Chhotu,

I was lost , didnt had any motivtion to study. You came and insppired me to work hard.
You came and I studied just to clear your doubts when you ask me.
I was jealous when you talked to other guys for your doubts,
I studied myself and didnt gave you opportunity to go to others.
You changed the way I used to live, made me a better person.
You enjoyed with me in my best moments and never left me in my worst.
Today I cant let you go without telling you how much I owe to you.
I may be angry, I may say words. I may not talk to you.
But nothing can replace that special place which you acquired in my heart.
And trust me nothing in my life gave me more joy than what I used to get in seeing your
reactions on surprising you with little moments.
Those were the best of the days.
You were, you are and you only will ALWAYS be my the best of the friend.

Yes You only, Chhotu.

You Chhotu,

You are one of a kind. Like a chocolate wrapped in mango pickle. Sweet yet so violent at times. But you are the reason that I am here and I cant thank you much.
